How to Make Amazing Peach Cobbler

In this tutorial, I will be sharing my family's recipe to make tasty peach cobbler.

Materials and Ingredients

To make this peach cobbler recipe, you will need:

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of canned peaches in 100% fruit juice (15 oz each)
  • 1 box of Pillsury pie crust. (This can be substituted for homemade pie crust or the brand you like)
  • 1 stick of salted butter
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp vanilla Extract
  • a small amount of lemon juice
  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream (optional)

Supplies

  • a baking pan
  • a large pot
  • a mixing spoon
  • a clean surface

Putting it all together:

  1. Drain your cans of peaches, and remember to store some of the juice from the can aside.
  2. Add two cans of drained peaches into a large pot. Add your stick of butter as well.
  3. Turn your pot onto low heat, until the butter is melted.
  4. Once the butter melts, add in cinnamon, nutmeg, brown sugar, vanilla extract and salt into the pot, and stir. Allow the pot to boil.
  5. While you are waiting, mix the peach juice and flour together to make a starchy liquid mixture.
  6. Add the liquid mixture into the pot.
  7. Add a squirt of lemon juice.
  8. Allow the peaches to cook in the syrup for 10 minutes, or until it is a thick consistency.
  9. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.
  10. Add melted butter to the surface and sides of the a medium sized baking pan.
  11. Add a sheet of your dough to cover the surface and sides of the baking pan.
  12. Add your peaches and the amount of syrup you prefer to the baking pan.
  13. Cover the peaches with another sheet of dough. Add some slits to the surface.
  14. Place baking pan in the oven. Allow to cook for 30-45 minutes or until the crust reaches a golden brown.
  15. Let cool and serve with a scoop of ice cream.
